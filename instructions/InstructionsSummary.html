Instructions

Summary

  • There will be one Assigner, 4 Green Solvers, and 4 Blue Solvers. And Solvers will be divided into Type 1 Solvers and Type 2 Solvers
  • All Green Solvers and 2 randomly selected Blue Solvers are Type 1 Solvers
  • Remaining 2 Blue Solvers are Type 2 Solvers
  • These roles are randomly assigned, but the same in every round
  • Solvers will participate in multiplication activities for 30 seconds in each round
  • Type 1 Solvers are always eligible to participate in multiplication Activities
  • Each Type 1 Solver decides whether to make the Type 2 solvers eligible to participate in the multiplication activities
  • The decision of one randomly chosen Type 1 Solver will be implemented
  • The assigner assigns eligible Solvers to Activity A or Activity B
  • Assigned solvers answer multiplication questions (Activity A or Activity B) for 30 seconds
  • For Type 1 solvers in Activity A each activity involves finding the product of two randomly chosen single-digit numbers and Solvers earn .50 points (half a point) for each activity
  • For Type 2 solvers in Activity A each activity involves finding the product of two randomly chosen single-digit numbers three times and earn 0.50 points (a quarter point) for each activity
  • For Type 1 solvers in Activity B each activity involves finding the product of two randomly chosen single-digit numbers and earn 0.25 (a quarter point) points for each activity
  • For Type 2 solvers in Activity B each activity involves finding the product of two randomly chosen single-digit numbers and earn 0.25 points (a quarter point) for each activity
  • During payment, the employer's points will be equivalent to dollars, while each employee's points will be multiplied by 3 to determine the corresponding dollar amounts.
